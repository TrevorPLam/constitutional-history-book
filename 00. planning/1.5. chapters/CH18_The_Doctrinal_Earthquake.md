# Chapter 18: The Doctrinal Earthquake (2021–2023)

### Chapter Overview
This chapter examines the Roberts Court's revolutionary transformation of American constitutional law through a coordinated series of landmark decisions. The doctrinal earthquake represents the most ambitious use of judicial supremacy in American history, fundamentally restructuring rights, equality, and democratic governance in just three years.

### Core Argument
The doctrinal earthquake demonstrates judicial supremacy's capacity for revolutionary transformation when combined with political power. By overturning decades of precedent across multiple domains simultaneously, the Court shows how judicial authority can achieve fundamental constitutional change when aligned with political support, completing the conservative project's ultimate goals.
