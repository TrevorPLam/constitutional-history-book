# Chapter 18: The Doctrinal Earthquake (2021–2023)

## Overview

**Time Span:** 2021–2023 (2 years)

**One-Paragraph Description:**
With a 6-3 supermajority, the Roberts Court reshapes American law with unprecedented speed. _Dobbs_ (2022) overrules _Roe_, eliminating the constitutional right to abortion. _Students for Fair Admissions v. Harvard_ (2023) ends race-conscious admissions, completing the shift to anticlassification. Justice Thomas’s _Dobbs_ concurrence signals that other privacy-based rights (contraception, gay rights) are under threat. Public trust collapses, with confidence reaching historic lows (25%). Meanwhile, a state constitutional counter-mobilization begins, with seven states amending their own constitutions to protect rights where the federal court has withdrawn.

## Driving Question

What happens when a 6-3 supermajority governs without restraint?

## Lenses Deployed

- **Primary Lens (Democracy vs. Supremacy):** 6-3 majority governs despite public pushback.
- **Secondary Lens 1 (Legitimacy):** Gallup 25% confidence; partisan chasm.
- **Secondary Lens 2 (Race):** _SFFA_ ends affirmative action; formal colorblindness triumphant.
- **Supporting Lens (Rights Cycles):** Aggressive retrenchment phase.
- **Supporting Lens (Gender):** _Dobbs_ exposes the lack of explicit constitutional gender protection.
- **Supporting Lens (Federalism):** Post-_Dobbs_ state divergence; state constitutionalism.

## Backlash Cycle: Dobbs and State Counter-Mobilization

| Beat                 | Event                                                              | Outcome                                                        |
| :------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Decision**         | Dobbs (2022) overrules Roe.                                        | Court eliminates 50-year-old constitutional right.             |
| **Reaction**         | Immediate state divergence; 13 state bans; record legitimacy drop. | Court becomes electorally toxic; national protests.            |
| **Counter-Reaction** | 7 states pass constitutional protect amendments (2022-2024).       | State constitutionalism emerges as counter-supremacy strategy. |
