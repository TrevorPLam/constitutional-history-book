# CH05: Legal Innovation: Developing Constitutional Doctrines for New Amendments

## Overview
The early Reconstruction Court developed innovative constitutional doctrines to interpret and enforce the new Reconstruction amendments, creating legal frameworks for protecting civil rights and voting rights in the post-war constitutional order.

## Primary Sources
### Doctrinal Development
- **Equal Protection Doctrine**: Court developed constitutional meaning of equal protection clause.
- **Due Process Innovation**: Judicial interpretation of substantive due process rights.
- **State Action Doctrine**: Legal framework for federal protection against state infringement.

### Constitutional Interpretation
- **14th Amendment Jurisprudence**: Court developed comprehensive interpretation of new amendment.
- **15th Amendment Enforcement**: Legal doctrines for protecting voting rights.
- **Federal Authority Expansion**: Judicial recognition of expanded federal power to protect rights.
