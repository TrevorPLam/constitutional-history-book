# CH05: The Legacy: Unfulfilled Constitutional Promise and Democratic Setback

## Overview
The end of Reconstruction represented an unfulfilled constitutional promise and a major democratic setback, as the revolutionary potential of the Reconstruction amendments was abandoned in favor of white supremacist restoration.

## Primary Sources
### Unfulfilled Promise
- **Constitutional Revolution Abandoned**: The revolutionary promise of equal citizenship remained unfulfilled.
- **Democratic Potential Wasted**: Opportunity for truly democratic American society was lost.
- **Civil Rights Deferred**: Federal protection of civil rights was postponed for nearly a century.

### Democratic Setback
- **White Supremacy Restored**: Legal and political restoration of white supremacist control.
- **Black Citizenship Limited**: Formal citizenship without effective civil rights protection.
- **Democratic Participation Restricted**: Systematic disenfranchisement of Black citizens.
