# CH05: Revolutionary Purpose: Transforming American Democracy and Citizenship

## Overview
The Reconstruction Amendments served a revolutionary purpose: transforming American democracy from a limited, racially exclusive system into a universal, egalitarian democracy with equal citizenship for all persons born in the United States.

## Primary Sources
### Democratic Transformation
- **Universal Citizenship**: 14th Amendment established birthright citizenship for all persons.
- **Equal Protection**: Constitutional guarantee of equal legal protection regardless of race.
- **Political Participation**: 15th Amendment guaranteed voting rights for Black male citizens.

### Revolutionary Vision
- **Radical Equality**: Complete legal and political equality for formerly enslaved persons.
- **Federal Protection**: National government empowered to protect individual rights against states.
- **Democratic Inclusion**: Full participation in American political and civic life.
