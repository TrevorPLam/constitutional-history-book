# CH05: The Consequences: Return to White Supremacist Control in the South

## Overview
The Supreme Court's systematic dismantling of Reconstruction protections enabled the return of white supremacist control in the South, effectively ending Black political participation and civil rights protection.

## Primary Sources
### White Supremacist Control
- **Political Disenfranchisement**: Court decisions enabled Southern states to disenfranchise Black voters.
- **Violence Impunity**: Limited federal prosecution of racial violence and intimidation.
- **Legal Segregation**: Court decisions laid foundation for Jim Crow segregation laws.

### Democratic Regression
- **Black Political Participation**: Dramatic reduction in Black voting and office-holding.
- **Civil Rights Regression**: Systematic erosion of Reconstruction-era civil rights gains.
- **White Supremacy Restored**: Legal and political restoration of white supremacist control.
