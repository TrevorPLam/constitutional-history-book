# CH05: The Opportunity: Using Judicial Supremacy for Democratic Ends

## Overview
The Reconstruction Amendments presented an unprecedented opportunity to use judicial supremacy for democratic rather than anti-democratic ends. The Court could enforce constitutional guarantees of equality and protect vulnerable minorities through its power of constitutional interpretation.

## Primary Sources
### Democratic Opportunity
- **Judicial Protection**: Court could enforce equal protection and due process guarantees.
- **Minority Rights**: Judicial power could protect Black citizens from state oppression.
- **Constitutional Enforcement**: Court's supremacy could advance rather than limit democracy.

### Transformative Potential
- **Rights Enforcement**: Judicial review could ensure state compliance with Reconstruction amendments.
- **Equality Protection**: Court could enforce constitutional guarantees of equal citizenship.
- **Democratic Safeguards**: Judicial power could protect democratic participation for all citizens.
