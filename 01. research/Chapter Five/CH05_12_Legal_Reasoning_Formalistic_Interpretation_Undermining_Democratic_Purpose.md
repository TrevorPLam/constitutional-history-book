# CH05: Legal Reasoning: Formalistic Interpretation Undermining Democratic Purpose

## Overview
The Supreme Court's formalistic interpretation of the 14th Amendment in the Slaughterhouse Cases undermined the amendment's democratic purpose of protecting civil rights and ensuring racial equality.

## Primary Sources
### Formalistic Reasoning
- **Technical Construction**: Court employed narrow, technical reading of constitutional text.
- **Distinction Creation**: Artificial separation between national and state citizenship rights.
- **Form Over Substance**: Legal formalism defeated amendment's substantive purpose.

### Democratic Purpose Undermined
- **Civil Rights Protection**: Formalistic interpretation limited federal protection of individual rights.
- **Racial Equality**: Decision undermined amendment's goal of racial equality.
- **Democratic Participation**: Technical reasoning restricted democratic participation guarantees.
