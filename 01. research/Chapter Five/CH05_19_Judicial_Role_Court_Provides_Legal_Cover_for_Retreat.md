# CH05: Judicial Role: Court Provides Legal Cover for Retreat

## Overview
The Supreme Court provided constitutional and legal justification for the retreat from Reconstruction, giving judicial legitimacy to the abandonment of civil rights protection and the restoration of white supremacist control.

## Primary Sources
### Legal Justification
- **Constitutional Rationale**: Court decisions provided constitutional basis for abandoning Reconstruction.
- **Legal Cover**: Judicial rulings gave legal legitimacy to Northern retreat and Southern resistance.
- **Institutional Support**: Supreme Court facilitated the political compromise ending Reconstruction.

### Constitutional Betrayal
- **Amendment Undermining**: Court systematically undermined Reconstruction amendments' purpose.
- **Federal Authority Limited**: Judicial decisions restricted federal power to protect civil rights.
- **State Authority Expanded**: Court provided constitutional justification for expanded state control.
