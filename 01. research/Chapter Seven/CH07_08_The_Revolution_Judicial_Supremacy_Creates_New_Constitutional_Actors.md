# CH07: The Revolution: Judicial Supremacy Creates New Constitutional Actors

## Overview
The Supreme Court's recognition of corporate personhood created a constitutional revolution, establishing corporations as new constitutional actors and fundamentally transforming the relationship between economic power and democratic governance.

## Primary Sources
### Constitutional Revolution
- **New Constitutional Actors**: Corporations became participants in constitutional politics.
- **Judicial Supremacy**: Court asserted authority to create constitutional rights.
- **Economic Power Constitutionalized**: Business interests received constitutional protection.

### Legal Framework
- **Corporate Constitutional Status**: Corporations gained standing in constitutional litigation.
- **Business Political Power**: Corporate entities gained constitutional political influence.
- **Democratic Regulation Limited**: Government power to regulate business constitutionally constrained.
