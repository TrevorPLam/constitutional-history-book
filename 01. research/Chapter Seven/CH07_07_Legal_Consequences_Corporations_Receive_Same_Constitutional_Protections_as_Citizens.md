# CH07: Legal Consequences: Corporations Receive Same Constitutional Protections as Citizens

## Overview
The legal consequences of corporate personhood were profound, as corporations received the same constitutional protections as human citizens, fundamentally altering the balance between business power and democratic regulation.

## Primary Sources
### Constitutional Equality
- **Corporate Rights Expansion**: Corporations gained full constitutional protections.
- **Citizen Parity**: Corporate entities treated as constitutional equals to citizens.
- **Business Liberty Protected**: Corporate economic freedom constitutionally guaranteed.

### Legal Framework
- **Equal Protection Application**: Corporations covered by equal protection clause.
- **Due Process Rights**: Corporate property and contract rights protected.
- **Regulatory Immunity**: Government regulation constitutionally limited.
