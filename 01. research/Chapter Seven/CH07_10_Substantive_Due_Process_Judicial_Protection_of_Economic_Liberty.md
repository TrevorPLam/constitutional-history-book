# CH07: Substantive Due Process: Judicial Protection of Economic Liberty

## Overview
The Supreme Court developed substantive due process doctrine to protect economic liberty, using the 14th Amendment's due process clause to shield business interests and strike down government regulation of the economy.

## Primary Sources
### Substantive Due Process Development
- **Economic Liberty Protection**: Court used due process to protect economic interests.
- **Business Rights Shield**: Corporate economic freedom constitutionally protected.
- **Regulatory Barriers**: Government regulation of economy constitutionally blocked.

### Legal Framework
- **Due Process Expansion**: Due process clause applied to economic regulation.
- **Liberty of Contract**: Freedom to contract protected as fundamental liberty.
- **Economic Due Process**: Judicial protection of business interests through constitutional interpretation.
