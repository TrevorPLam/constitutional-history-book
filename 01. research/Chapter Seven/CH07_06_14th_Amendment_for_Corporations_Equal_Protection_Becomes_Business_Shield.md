# CH07: 14th Amendment for Corporations: Equal Protection Becomes Business Shield

## Overview
The Supreme Court extended 14th Amendment equal protection to corporations, transforming the amendment designed to protect freed slaves into a shield for business interests against government regulation.

## Primary Sources
### 14th Amendment Extension
- **Equal Protection for Corporations**: Court applied equal protection clause to corporate entities.
- **Business Shield**: Amendment used to protect corporate interests from regulation.
- **Constitutional Irony**: Civil rights amendment became business protection tool.

### Legal Framework
- **Corporate Constitutional Rights**: Corporations gained same protections as citizens.
- **Regulatory Barriers**: Equal protection blocked government regulation of business.
- **Business Liberty**: Corporate economic freedom constitutionally protected.
