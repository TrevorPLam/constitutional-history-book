# CH04: Constitutional Revolution: 13th, 14th, 15th Amendments Overturn Dred Scott

## Overview
The Reconstruction Amendments (13th, 14th, and 15th) represented a constitutional revolution that completely overturned the Dred Scott decision and transformed American constitutional law.

## Primary Sources
### Constitutional Amendments
- **13th Amendment (1865)**: Abolished slavery throughout the United States.
- **14th Amendment (1868)**: Established birthright citizenship and equal protection.
- **15th Amendment (1870)**: Guaranteed voting rights regardless of race.

### Dred Scott Overturn
- **Citizenship Reversal**: 14th Amendment granted citizenship to all persons born in U.S.
- **Equal Protection**: Established constitutional basis for civil rights.
- **Federal Authority**: Confirmed congressional power to protect civil rights.
