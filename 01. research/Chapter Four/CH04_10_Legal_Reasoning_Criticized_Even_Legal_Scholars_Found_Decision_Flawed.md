# CH04: Legal Reasoning Criticized: Even Legal Scholars Found Decision Flawed

## Overview
The Dred Scott decision faced widespread criticism from legal scholars who found its reasoning fundamentally flawed. Even supporters of judicial review acknowledged the decision's legal deficiencies and poor constitutional analysis.

## Primary Sources
### Legal Criticism
- **Academic Response**: Law professors and scholars condemned the reasoning.
- **Legal Journals**: Contemporary legal publications criticized the decision.
- **Professional Opinion**: Members of the legal bar questioned the decision's quality.

### Constitutional Flaws
- **Historical Analysis**: Poor understanding of constitutional history and precedent.
- **Logical Errors**: Inconsistent reasoning and contradictory conclusions.
- **Methodological Problems**: Flawed approach to constitutional interpretation.
