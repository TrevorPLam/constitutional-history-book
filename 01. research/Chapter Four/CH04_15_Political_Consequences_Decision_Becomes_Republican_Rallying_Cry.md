# CH04: Political Consequences: Decision Becomes Republican Rallying Cry

## Overview
The Dred Scott decision became a powerful rallying cry for the newly formed Republican Party, providing political ammunition that helped transform the party from a minor faction into a national political force.

## Primary Sources
### Republican Mobilization
- **Political Platform**: Decision featured prominently in Republican platforms.
- **Campaign Rhetoric**: Republican leaders used decision to mobilize voters.
- **Party Growth**: Decision accelerated Republican expansion across Northern states.

### Political Transformation
- **Lincoln's Rise**: Decision catapulted Lincoln to national prominence.
- **Sectional Alignment**: Decision hardened North-South political divisions.
- **Electoral Impact**: Decision influenced 1858 and 1860 elections.
