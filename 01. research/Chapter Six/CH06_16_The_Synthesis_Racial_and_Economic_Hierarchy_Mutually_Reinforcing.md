# CH06: The Synthesis: Racial and Economic Hierarchy Mutually Reinforcing

## Overview
The Supreme Court's constitutional synthesis created a mutually reinforcing system of racial and economic hierarchy, using the same legal doctrines to protect white supremacy and corporate power while abandoning racial justice and worker protection.

## Primary Sources
### Hierarchical Synthesis
- **Dual Hierarchy Protection**: Court simultaneously protected racial and economic hierarchies.
- **Mutual Reinforcement**: White supremacy and corporate power reinforced each other constitutionally.
- **Systematic Logic**: Consistent constitutional logic applied across racial and economic domains.

### Constitutional Framework
- **14th Amendment Dual Application**: Amendment protected both corporate personhood and private racial discrimination.
- **Due Process Expansion**: Substantive due process protected economic interests while enabling racial oppression.
- **Equal Protection Inversion**: Equal protection clause applied to corporations but not to Black citizens.
