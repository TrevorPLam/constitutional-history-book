# CH06: The Pattern: Economic Power Receives Same Protection as White Supremacy

## Overview
The Supreme Court established a clear pattern of using constitutional interpretation to protect both racial and economic hierarchies, with the 14th Amendment shielding white supremacy and corporate power while abandoning racial justice and worker protection.

## Primary Sources
### Dual Protection Pattern
- **Racial Hierarchy Protection**: Court protected white supremacist control through constitutional interpretation.
- **Economic Hierarchy Protection**: Court shielded corporate power and business interests from regulation.
- **Consistent Logic**: Same constitutional doctrines applied to protect different forms of hierarchy.

### Constitutional Framework
- **14th Amendment Dual Use**: Amendment protected both corporate personhood and private discrimination.
- **Due Process Expansion**: Substantive due process protected economic interests while limiting racial justice.
- **Equal Protection Inversion**: Equal protection clause applied to corporations but not to Black citizens.
