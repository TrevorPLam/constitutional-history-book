# CH06: Judicial Opportunity: Court as Agent of Counter-Revolution

## Overview
The Supreme Court seized the opportunity presented by Northern retreat, Southern resistance, and economic transformation to become the primary agent of counter-revolution against Reconstruction's constitutional promise.

## Primary Sources
### Judicial Opportunity
- **Political Vacuum**: Court filled void left by Northern abandonment.
- **Constitutional Authority**: Court used judicial supremacy to reverse Reconstruction.
- **Counter-Revolution Role**: Court became primary instrument of racial and economic hierarchy restoration.

### Institutional Transformation
- **From Protector to Betrayer**: Court transformed from civil rights protector to hierarchy enforcer.
- **Constitutional Reinterpretation**: Court reinterpreted Reconstruction amendments to serve opposite purposes.
- **Judicial Supremacy Applied**: Court used power to enforce rather than challenge hierarchy.
