# CH06: Procedural Barriers: Voting Rights Undermined Through Technical Requirements

## Overview
The Supreme Court enabled the systematic disenfranchisement of Black citizens through procedural barriers and technical requirements that appeared neutral but effectively eliminated Black political participation.

## Primary Sources
### Procedural Barriers
- **Poll Taxes**: Court upheld voting taxes as constitutional requirements.
- **Literacy Tests**: Court approved literacy tests as voter qualification measures.
- **Grandfather Clauses**: Court sanctioned racial exemptions designed to maintain white voting dominance.

### Technical Requirements
- **Registration Barriers**: Complex voter registration processes upheld as constitutional.
- **Residency Requirements**: Lengthy residency requirements validated as voter qualifications.
- **Administrative Discretion**: Local officials given broad discretion in voter qualification decisions.
