# CH06: The Betrayal Complete: Reconstruction Promise Officially Destroyed

## Overview
Plessy v. Ferguson completed the Supreme Court's betrayal of Reconstruction, officially destroying the constitutional promise of racial equality and establishing Jim Crow segregation as constitutional law.

## Primary Sources
### Complete Betrayal
- **Reconstruction Promise Destroyed**: Court officially abandoned Reconstruction's constitutional vision.
- **Jim Crow Constitutionalized**: Segregation and discrimination received constitutional legitimacy.
- **Racial Justice Abandoned**: Federal protection of civil rights completely eliminated.

### Constitutional Revolution
- **14th Amendment Transformed**: Amendment changed from shield for equality to weapon for hierarchy.
- **Equal Protection Inverted**: Equal protection clause used to justify rather than prevent discrimination.
- **Federal Power Eliminated**: Federal government lost all constitutional authority to protect civil rights.
