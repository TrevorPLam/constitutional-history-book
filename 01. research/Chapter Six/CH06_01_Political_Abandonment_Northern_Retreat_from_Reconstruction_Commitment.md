# CH06: Political Abandonment: Northern Retreat from Reconstruction Commitment

## Overview
Northern political abandonment of Reconstruction created the context for judicial betrayal, as declining Northern commitment to racial equality enabled the Supreme Court to dismantle Reconstruction protections without political consequence.

## Primary Sources
### Northern Retreat
- **Political Will Declining**: Northern public opinion shifted away from Reconstruction support.
- **Economic Priorities**: Northern focus on industrialization over racial justice.
- **War Fatigue**: Growing Northern exhaustion with continued Southern intervention.

### Abandonment Mechanisms
- **Federal Withdrawal**: Reduction of federal troops and enforcement in the South.
- **Political Compromise**: Northern willingness to trade civil rights for political stability.
- **Public Indifference**: Northern apathy toward Southern racial violence and discrimination.
