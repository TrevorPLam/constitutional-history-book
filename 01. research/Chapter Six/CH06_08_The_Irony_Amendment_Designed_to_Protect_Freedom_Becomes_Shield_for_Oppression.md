# CH06: The Irony: Amendment Designed to Protect Freedom Becomes Shield for Oppression

## Overview
The supreme irony of the Civil Rights Cases was that the 14th Amendment, designed to protect Black freedom and equality, became a constitutional shield protecting private racial oppression and discrimination.

## Primary Sources
### Constitutional Irony
- **Purpose Reversed**: Amendment meant to protect freedom became protection for oppression.
- **Freedom Shielded**: Private discrimination received constitutional protection as "freedom."
- **Equality Undermined**: Amendment designed for equality became tool of inequality.

### Legal Transformation
- **Protective to Oppressive**: Constitutional protection transformed from shield to sword.
- **Freedom Redefined**: Private "freedom" to discriminate protected over public equality.
- **Equality Subverted**: Constitutional equality subverted to protect private inequality.
