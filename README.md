# Constitutional History Book Project

A comprehensive research project on the development of American constitutional history, focusing on the evolution of judicial power and institutional authority from the founding era through the Civil War amendments.

## Project Structure

- **00. planning/** - Initial planning documents and chapter outlines
- **01. research/** - Detailed research materials organized by chapter
- **02. chapters/** - Draft chapters in progress
- **chapters/** - Final chapter versions

## Research Focus

This project examines:
- The constitutional judiciary's fragile beginnings
- Marshall's strategic gambit to establish judicial authority
- The Jacksonian challenge to judicial power
- The Civil War's constitutional legacy through the 13th, 14th, and 15th Amendments

## Usage

This repository contains research materials, drafts, and planning documents for a scholarly work on American constitutional development.
